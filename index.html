Process

From idea to production
in three steps.

01
Connect your repository

Link GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket in seconds. NexaFlow automatically detects your framework and configures optimal build settings.

02
Configure your pipeline

Define environments, set up secrets, and configure your deployment rules — all from a beautiful visual dashboard or plain YAML.
